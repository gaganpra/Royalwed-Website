Mehendi Artist

Shortly after, the Mehndi Ceremony begins. Mehndi Ceremony is basically where the family members of the bride and groom, come up on the stage, greet the bride and groom, put henna on the palm of their hands and feed them sweets. Sometimes, they would also drop money in a basket that is placed in front of them.

Application of mehndi can prevent too much stress because it cools the body and keeps the nerves from becoming tense. This is the reason why mehndi is applied on the hands and feet, which house nerve endings in the body. Also, being a highly antiseptic agent, mehndi can protect the couple from viral diseases.
